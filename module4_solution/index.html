Chicken

For some reason, more and more people are keeping chickens as pets. Some are even using them as comfort animals. What is it about chickens that people are finding so appealing? I've kept pet birds and think that, perhaps, the thing is that chickens are birds just like parrots and finches. Their song is not nearly as pretty, but they can be very friendly and hopelessly devoted. Too bad they taste so good.
